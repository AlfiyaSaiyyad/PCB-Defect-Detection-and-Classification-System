About Our PCB Defect Detection System

Our system leverages state-of-the-art EfficientNet-B0 Deep Learning architecture to provide automated visual inspection of Printed Circuit Boards with up to 99% accuracy.

How It Works

The AI analyzes pixel-level patterns in the PCB image, comparing them against thousands of trained samples to identify structural irregularities.

Key Features

  • 6 Class Detection: Short, Missing Hole, etc.
  • Real-time Speed: Results in < 1 second.
  • High Precision: Minimizes human error.

Steps to Scan

  1. Upload a clear top-down PCB image.
  2. Click "Start AI Inspection" to start AI analysis.
  3. Review the defect label and confidence score.
